FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Healyx work?

Healyx simplifies healthcare by offering services like online consultations, medicine delivery, and home care. You can book an appointment with a healthcare professional, consult online, receive a digital prescription, and have medicines or additional medical support delivered right to your home.

What types of healthcare professionals are available through Healyx?

Healyx offers access to a wide range of healthcare professionals, including general practitioners, specialists, and medical care providers such as nurses or compounders. You can schedule appointments for both in-person and online consultations.

How does medicine delivery work with Healyx?

After your consultation, Healyx arranges for your prescribed medications to be delivered directly to your home. This service eliminates the need to visit a pharmacy, providing added convenience and ensuring that you have everything you need for your treatment.

Can I get follow-up care after my consultation through Healyx?

Yes! Healyx offers on-demand services where you can book home care services, such as nursing support or assistance with medical devices, for post-consultation needs or regular check-ups.

How do I pay for Healyx services?

Healyx will offer multiple payment options for your convenience, including online payment via credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and health insurance integration where applicable. You can pay after booking an appointment or once your service is completed.
